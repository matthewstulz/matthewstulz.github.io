Who am I?

Dial-up to Fiber Optic

Being an avid tech enthusiest left me embracing the sounds of the dial-up scream while I was stuck looking in from beyond the grid. While others enjoyed online gaming or surfing the world wide web from webpage to website, I sat patiently while a youtube video took an eternity to buffer. This left me hungry and filled with angst.

To other generations, this would seem like a "first world problem". To a young teenager from Kentucky, the struggle was real.

Fueled with the desire to create. Fueled with the ambition to make something great. Fueled with the obsession to leave a story to inspire.

I am an explorer, an optimist, a creative mind, a developer, and someone who seeks the next challenge.

My Mission

The urge to create profitable software that improves the lives of the user base. Implementing new and groundbreaking ideas by trying alternative techniques, programming langauges, frameworks and staying in front of the trends.
